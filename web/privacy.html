Kemetic Calendar Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 13, 2026

Kemetic Calendar stores the data needed to run your calendar, reminders, reflections, flows, notes, journal entries, nutrition entries, profile, sharing features, and notification preferences. Some features can be used locally on your device; account-backed features sync data through our Supabase backend.

Data We Collect

When you create or use an account, we may store your account identifier, email address or provider sign-in identifier, profile details you provide, calendar and event content, reminders, journal and reflection content, flow content, shared-calendar membership, posts or comments you create, device push tokens, diagnostic timestamps, and settings needed to keep the app working.

How We Use Data

We use this data to authenticate your account, sync your content, deliver reminders and push notifications, support sharing features, restore app state, protect the service, troubleshoot support requests, and improve reliability. We do not sell personal data and we do not use third-party advertising tracking.

Sharing

Data is shared only as needed to provide the service, such as with Supabase for authentication and storage, Firebase for push delivery, Apple or Google services when you choose device-calendar or sign-in integrations, and other Kemetic Calendar users when you intentionally share calendar or flow content.

Retention and Deletion

Account data is kept while your account is active unless a shorter retention period applies to a specific feature. You can request deletion from Settings in the app by choosing Delete account, or by visiting maat.app/delete-account. Deletion removes your account sign-in and account data that we are not required to retain for legal, security, or fraud-prevention reasons.

Your Choices

You can disable push notifications in the app or in device settings. You can unlink device-calendar sync from Settings. You can also contact us to ask questions about your data or deletion request.

Contact

Email support@maat.app for privacy or support requests.
